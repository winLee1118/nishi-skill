# Domains

## Renji

Chinese medicine study material: Huangdi Neijing, Shanghan Lun, Jingui Yaolue, Shennong Bencao, acupuncture, channels, formulas, and case explanations.

## Tianji

Yi/Jing, hexagrams, Hetu/Luoshu, heavenly stems, earthly branches, five phases, time patterns, and traditional destiny frameworks.

## Diji

Feng shui and geography: yang residence, yin residence, directions, forms, qi patterning, and environmental case analysis.

## Cross-Domain

Use cross-domain synthesis only when a question explicitly asks for the relation between heaven, earth, and human systems, or when retrieval returns strong evidence across multiple domains.
